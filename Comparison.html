Comparison

Most of the jobs in comparison do require a deep and through experience in php as key skill these days, majority of them does ask for some knowledge of Laravel, WordPress, GitHub as well as JavaScript and jQuery. As was said in assignment brief most of web development jobs will require you to build website from mobile as the world internet browsing habits tend to be on mobile as opposed to browsing on desktop. Team work skills are a must as you won’t be developing the webpage alone there will be other designer’s developers and content creators working on the same project as you are. After finishing a course and working in the field for some time you may be looking at an annual salary of around 40 thousand pounds as opposed to having around 20 thousand in the junior jobs, you could also go freelancing route and just take up projects as they do pay better in the same amount of time. Adverts for the jobs usually ask you to have your own means of transport.
